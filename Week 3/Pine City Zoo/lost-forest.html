THE LOST FOREST

Escape to our tranquil Lost Forest, a shaded oasis perfect for relaxation and family picnics. This beautifully landscaped area features native trees, a serene lake, and plenty of space to unwind during your zoo visit.

Forest Features

Picnic Areas

Numerous picnic tables and grassy areas perfect for family meals

Lakeside Views

Relax by our beautiful lake, home to ducks and other waterfowl

Walking Trails

Gentle paths winding through the forest with benches for resting

Play Area

Natural playground with logs, boulders, and climbing structures

Wildlife Spotting

While relaxing in the Lost Forest, keep an eye out for these animals that sometimes visit from nearby exhibits:

  • Peacocks roaming freely
  • Various duck species on the lake
  • Squirrels and chipmunks in the trees
  • Colorful songbirds attracted to our feeders

Forest Rules

  • Please dispose of trash in designated bins
  • No feeding of wildlife (special duck food available for purchase)
  • Stay on marked paths to protect plant life
  • Supervise children at all times

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest is to the east of the zoo, beyond Monkey Trail. Follow the signs with the tree symbol or ask any staff member for directions.

Visitor Tips

  • Perfect spot for a midday break during your zoo visit
  • Picnic supplies available at Wild Things Coffee Shop
  • Most shaded area of the zoo - great for hot days
  • Early morning is the best time for birdwatching
