MONKEY TRAIL

Challenge yourself on our exciting Monkey Trail, a unique adventure course that lets you experience the zoo from a whole new perspective. Whether you choose to walk or ride, you'll enjoy stunning views and close encounters with nature.

Trail Options

30-Minute Trail

Our shorter loop perfect for families with young children or those wanting a quick adventure. Includes:

  • Rope bridges and net climbs
  • Viewing platforms overlooking monkey exhibits
  • Interactive learning stations
1-Hour Adventure

The full Monkey Trail experience for those seeking more challenge. Adds:

  • Zipline section over a small ravine
  • Vertical climbing wall
  • Hidden forest path with wildlife spotting opportunities
  • Access to our canopy observation deck

Safety Information

  • Closed-toe shoes required
  • Maximum weight limit: 120kg (265lbs)
  • Minimum height for full trail: 1.2m (4ft)
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult
  • Trail may close during inclement weather

Wildlife You Might See

While on the trail, keep an eye out for these animals in their naturalistic habitats:

Dusky Leaf Monkeys
Two-toed Sloths
Colorful Toucans
Ring-tailed Lemurs

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail begins near the Insect House at the eastern side of the zoo. Look for the giant monkey statue at the entrance.

Visitor Tips

  • Wear comfortable clothing you don't mind getting dirty
  • Morning hours are less crowded and cooler
  • Lockers available for storing bags (small fee applies)
  • Bring a camera - the views are spectacular!
