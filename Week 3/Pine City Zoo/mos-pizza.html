WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Mo's Pizza is located in the main Food Court near the zoo entrance. Look for the red-and-white checkered tablecloths and the smell of fresh-baked pizza!

Visitor Tips

  • Peak lunch hours (12-1:30pm) can be busy - consider early or late lunch
  • Gluten-free crust available upon request
  • Combination meals available for zoo members
  • Ask about our "Build-a-Pizza" workshop for kids on weekends
