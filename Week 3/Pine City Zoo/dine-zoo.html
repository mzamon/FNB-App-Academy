DINE @THE ZOO

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Dine @The Zoo is located just behind the Amphitheatre, with views overlooking the giraffe enclosure. Follow the signs from the main plaza or ask any staff member for directions.

Visitor Tips

  • Ask about our daily specials featuring seasonal ingredients
  • Dress code is smart casual - no beachwear or athletic wear please
  • We accommodate most dietary restrictions with advance notice
  • Happy Hour is weekdays from 4-6pm with discounted drinks
